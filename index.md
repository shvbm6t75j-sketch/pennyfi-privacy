PennyFi Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

PennyFi is a personal finance app that keeps your data private. All your financial information is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced across your own devices via your private iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter into PennyFi — including accounts, transactions, budgets, payees, and investment holdings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device database. We do not operate any servers and have no ability to access your information.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, PennyFi uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only to you. We cannot read, access, or retrieve this data. Apple's iCloud privacy policy governs this storage.

Market Data

To display current investment prices and currency exchange rates, PennyFi makes requests to the following public data providers:

• Yahoo Finance (stock and ETF prices)
• CoinGecko (cryptocurrency prices)
• Frankfurter.app (currency exchange rates, sourced from the European Central Bank)

These requests contain only ticker symbols or currency codes — no personal or financial information is sent. We have no control over these third-party services and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Biometric Authentication

PennyFi offers an optional app lock using Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system and is never accessible to PennyFi.

Data Collection

PennyFi does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party SDKs. There are no user accounts or registration required.

Data Export and Deletion

You can export your data at any time as a backup file from within the app. You can delete all your data using the "Make a Fresh Start" option in Settings, which permanently removes all information from your device.

Children's Privacy

PennyFi does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
